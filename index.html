Getting to the Glassboat

As it’s in the centre of Bristol, parking is limited. The nearest carpark to the Glassboat is an NCP on Queen Charlotte St but it is ridiculously expensive so don’t go there unless you really need to! :) The best long-stay car park is Trenchard Street multi-storey BS1 5AN. It is open 24 hours and is only 10 mins walk from both venues.

Accessibility

Glassboat - The Glassboat is a static boat in the harbourside and is easily accessible for all. Although there are no disabled toilets on the boat, they have an arrangement with the Mercure Hotel opposite to use their facilities. Cars can pull up outside the front door however there is no parking directly outside the venue. Some on-street metered parking is available a few minutes away on Welshback and there is the pricey Queen Charlotte St NCP just around the corner if you do need to park close by.

St George’s - The main access to St George’s on Great George St is via a large flight of steps. There is disabled access via the small car park at the rear which is on Charlotte Street. There is limited space in the car park (only 4 spaces) which will be used by caterers/band/etc so if you require nearby parking, there is on-street metered parking just outside. Otherwise Trenchard Street parking is 10 mins walk away. To access the building from the car park, there is a lift next to the rear steps. Someone will be there to assist you. If you are going to arrive at St George’s via the rear car park, do make sure you drive down Great Georges St first as the front of the building is worth a look.

Confetti

Confetti needs to be something natural and easy to sweep up like real or dried petals. The paper type is not allowed as it's impossible to clean up. Please only use it on the steps at the front of St. George’s where it can be swept up easily.

Dietary requirements

Please let us know if you have any dietary requirements. Our caterers are really accommodating and will do their best to find an alternative if there is anything you don’t eat and/or are allergic to. Plus if you think your kids won’t want to eat the main food, our caterers said they can do pizza instead! Do let us know when you RSVP if you’d like this.
